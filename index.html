AI asks questions.

No answers until you've attempted it yourself. Like a coach, not a search engine.
🤝
MODE 02
Assist
You share thinking.
AI makes it better.
Your idea leads. AI refines and extends it. You stay the author.

MODE 03
Execute
Full AI. When you
actually need speed.
Available, but never the default. You have to choose it consciously.
